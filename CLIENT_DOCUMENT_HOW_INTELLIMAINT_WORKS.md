## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What documents should I upload?**
A: Any equipment manuals, maintenance guides, troubleshooting procedures, safety documentation, or training materials relevant to your business. PDFs work best.

**Q: How long does it take to upload and index a document?**
A: Upload is instant (1‚Äì2 seconds). Indexing takes 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on file size. Once indexed, it's searchable forever.

**Q: Can I upload classified or sensitive documents?**
A: Yes. Classified documents stay completely private within your team account. Only people you've invited have access.

**Q: What if my team is in the field with no internet?**
A: You can pre-download certain documents for offline access. Chat requires internet, but previously loaded documents work offline.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes. IntelliMaint works on phones, tablets, and computers. Voice mode is optimized for mobile field work.

**Q: How accurate are the answers?**
A: The accuracy depends on how complete and clear your documents are. If your manual says "Replace bolt with a 7mm wrench," the AI will tell your technician to use a 7mm wrench.

**Q: What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?**
A: The system will tell you honestly: "I couldn't find information about that in your documents." You can then upload the missing manual or ask a follow-up question.

**Q: Can I export chat histories for compliance?**
A: Yes. Export conversations as text or PDF for documentation, audits, or team review.

**Q: Who gets access to the admin dashboard?**
A: You choose. Assign managers/supervisors to the Admin role; they can then see team activity, uploads, and analytics.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Pricing varies by team size and storage needs. Contact us for a custom quote.

**Q: How do I get support?**
A: Email support@intellimaint.com with screenshots, descriptions, and your team name. We typically respond within 24 hours.
